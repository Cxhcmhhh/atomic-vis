Visualization of generated dance sequence

The model generate a typical instance of Jazz-ballet. The textual descriptions of the atomic movements are summarized by an LLM. However, each of these induced atomic movements corresponds to an actual existing dance primitive: palms showing and waving corresponds to the Jazz dance movement Jazz hands; pricked turns correspond to the jazz-ballet pique turns; walk corresponds to the Jazz dance Jazz walk; jump with one leg extending corresponds to the jazz-ballet jeté; spin on the ground is a typical floor roll; extend one leg corresponds to the jazz-ballet tilt.
